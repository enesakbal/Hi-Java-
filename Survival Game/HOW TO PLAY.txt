A Survival Game

Purpose of this game is to collect all of vital items . Vital items are food, water and firewood.
You have three class for our hero. classes are samurai, archer and knight.
the stats are damage, armor and money . and stats varies for each class.
You have five place. Places are safe house, river, forest, cave and store.
You must fight to enemies (zombie, vampire and bear) in river, forest or store. and you must collect a vital item. if you can collect all of vital items you win.
You can buy items in store with our money. items makes you more powerful.
